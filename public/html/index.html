Features

Real-time Whiteboard

Teachers can utilise the built-in board to communicate their ideas visually, and these changes are broadcasted to all students in the lesson.

Audio Broadcasting

Teachers do not need to rely on textual communication, with built-in audio broadcasting to all students using a microphone.

Quick Sharing

Classrooms can be shared easily by simply sharing a link, making it easy to use for both students and teachers.

Polling

Teachers can gauge the standard of their classes by seeing the scores from questions set on OnBoard.
